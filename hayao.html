Who is Hayao Miyazaki?

Renowned for co-founding Studio Ghibli, Hayao Miyazaki is a highly acclaimed anime director and producer. His films, including "Spirited Away," "My Neighbor Totoro," and "Princess Mononoke," are celebrated for their enchanting storytelling, strong environmental themes, and breathtaking animation. Miyazaki's contributions have earned him international recognition and numerous awards.
