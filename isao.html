Who is Isao Takahata?

Another co-founder of Studio Ghibli, Isao Takahata is a key figure in anime's history. He directed films like "Grave of the Fireflies" and "The Tale of the Princess Kaguya," showcasing a commitment to diverse storytelling and exploring mature themes. Takahata's influence extends beyond entertainment, emphasizing the artistic and emotional potential of anime.
